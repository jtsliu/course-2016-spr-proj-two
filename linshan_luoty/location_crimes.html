The distribution of crimes among Massachusetts

Source: Tom Carden, Gapminder.

This is a distribution of number of crimes of each zipcode of Massachusetts. Each circle shows how many crimes happened in that area. The x-axix is longitude, and y-axix is latitude, and each circle is drawn according to its specific position on the map.
